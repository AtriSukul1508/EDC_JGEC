ABOUT   US

MISSION:

  1. Give an exposure to the business world through well-coordinated business competitions and boot camp for start-ups presided over and mentored by established professionals
  2. Organising workshops, group discussions, elocutions, debates etc periodically to inculcate strong convincing skills, self believe and enhance general knowledge.
  3. We aim to create a strong entrepreneurship platform in the north Bengal by conducting large-scale programs like E-Summits and contribute substantially to the entrepreneurial growth of north Bengal
  4. Ingeniously use social media to create awareness about entrepreneurship.


VISION:

EDC JGEC believes that the word “entrepreneurship”, not only needs to be pronounced properly but also needs to be understood by each individual.
Our vision is to support and guide each blooming entrepreneurial idea through a pathway of opportunities and enhancement. We intend to develop and motivate young minds to think with a businesslike approach, cultivate creativity, confidence and resolute motivation, not only to be successful entrepreneurs but also to develop their personalities.
We want to be a well recognized centre of excellence for entrepreneurship development.

VALUES:

  1. Ideas are meant to be explored and experimented.
  2. Taking criticism positively assist in self development.
  3. Fear needs to be overcome and not suppressed.
  4. There are sample ways of gaining knowledge if one has the zeal, sharing knowledge helps in escalating.
